Main story events and progression.
MAIN STORY OUTLINE

ACT 1: Death & Deal
- Player is hit by a truck
- System introduces Love Point survival rules
- Player enters the otome world

ACT 2: Academy Life
- Player begins final year at Academia Fortunae
- Encounters love interests
- Faces discrimination as a commoner

ACT 3: Trials & Exams
- Five major exams using logic and puzzles
- Exams reward bonus Love Points
- Affection events occur after each exam

ACT 4: Emotional Climax
- Secrets of love interests revealed
- Player chooses who to prioritize

ACT 5: Graduation & Endings
- Ending determined by affection levels
- Optional return to real world using Elixir of Life
