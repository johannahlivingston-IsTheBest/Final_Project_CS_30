Player perspective story and branching choices.

PLAYER CHARACTER

Real World Background:
The player lived an ordinary life for 30 years and never experienced romantic love. While distracted by reading an otome game synopsis, they were struck by a truck.

System Introduction:
Upon awakening in a hospital, a system window appears:

“Congratulations, Player! You are dying.
The good news? Higher beings have chosen you for entertainment.
The bad news? Your survival depends on Love Points.”

Player Identity:
If male: Felix
If female: Amelia

Appearance:
Curly red hair, forest green eyes, striking features that attract attention despite commoner status.

In-Game Role:
A struggling commoner with newly awakened healing magic. Admitted to Academia Fortunae on a scholarship for their final year.

Internal Conflict:
The player must survive in a world where affection equals life, while questioning whether love gained under pressure is real.

Special Trait:
The player alone can view and influence Love Points directly.
