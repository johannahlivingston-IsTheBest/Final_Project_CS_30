Overview:
This world operates on a visible affection-based system known as Love Points. Emotions are quantifiable, fate is adjustable, and survival depends on maintaining meaningful connections.

The player awakens in this world after a fatal truck accident in their original reality. They are offered a chance to survive by completing the final year at the Royal Academy, Academia Fortunae.

Rules of the World:
1. Love Points determine survival.
2. If all Love Points reach zero, the player dies.
3. Graduation from Academia Fortunae is mandatory.
4. Forming bonds unlocks endings.
5. Higher beings observe the player for entertainment purposes.

Academia Fortunae:
A prestigious academy attended by royalty, nobility, and rare magic users. Commoners are almost never admitted, making the player an anomaly.

Themes:
- Survival through connection
- Choice vs manipulation
- Love as currency
- Self-worth
